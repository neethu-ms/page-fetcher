Corporate Research Associates (CRA) is a renowned market research and intelligence firm.

Always evolving and innovating, we provide a full spectrum of qualitative and quantitative research services and strategic consultation.

We talk to people. We unlock the meaning behind their thoughts and opinions, and transform data into understanding. How do we do this? Through standard and innovative methodologies, in-depth analysis and advanced analytics, and by providing comprehensive and interactive reporting.

At CRA, we have one of the largest contingents of Certified Marketing Research Professionals in Canada. With expertise across all sectors, we deliver services in both official languages.

We serve public, private sector, and non-profit organizations throughout Canada and abroad.
